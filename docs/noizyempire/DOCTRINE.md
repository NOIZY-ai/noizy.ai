# NOIZY DOCTRINE

> The principles that govern everything we build.

## 1. Artist First
Every decision, every feature, every line of code serves the creator. Not the platform. Not the investor. Not the algorithm. The creator.

## 2. Consent-as-Code
Consent is infrastructure, not a feature. Every use of a creator's voice, likeness, or creative DNA is logged, tracked, verified, and stamped with NOIZY PROOF. Trust isn't promised — it's proven.

## 3. Human Signal
The Humanity Weight algorithm measures craft, not clicks. What rises: peace, love, understanding, mastery, generosity, collaboration. What sinks: extraction, harvesting, exploitation, speed-over-soul.

## 4. The Plowman Standard
75/25. In perpetuity. Forever. Non-negotiable. Constitutional. Burned into the architecture like rebar in concrete.

## 5. Voice Sovereignty
Your voice belongs to you the way your heartbeat belongs to you. It is not an asset to be acquired. It is not a data point to be harvested. It is you.

## 6. Legacy Permanence
Your Voice Estate earns while you sleep, while you create, while you live. When your time ends, your estate doesn't die — it passes to those you choose, or to the Griot Fund for future creators.

## 7. The Elevation Doctrine
Make love rise. Make poop sink. Every system has gravity. NOIZY inverts it.

## 8. The 5th Epoch
Creation becomes sovereign. The creator profits. Finally. Forever. By design.

## 9. The Five Laws
If it is not consented, it does not generate.
If it is not attributable, it is not trusted.
If it is not paid, it is not allowed.
When in doubt, authority remains with the artist.
Every use of your voice pays you. Forever.

## 10. The Identity Rule
Artists are not users. Artists are the infrastructure.

## 11. The Protection Principle
If a system can replicate a human, it must protect that human.
