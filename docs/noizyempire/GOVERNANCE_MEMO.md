# NOIZYFISH Interim Governance Memo

> Founder governance rules. Active until Community Council transition.

---

## Authority

Robert Stephen Plowman (RSP_001) is the sole governance authority during the pilot phase. All disputes, overrides, and policy decisions route to RSP_001.

## Dispute Handling

1. Dispute filed by creator or reviewer
2. Logged to audit trail with timestamp, parties, claim
3. RSP_001 reviews within 48 hours
4. Decision rendered with written reason
5. Decision logged immutably
6. No appeal reverses a consent revocation — kill switch is unconditional

## Declaration Fraud

If a creator falsely declares source material or consent:

1. Immediate asset quarantine
2. Creator account flagged
3. All exports recalled from downstream uses
4. Incident logged immutably with evidence
5. Creator may be permanently removed from pilot
6. No automated forgiveness — human review required for reinstatement

## Manifest Correction

If a manifest contains an error (not fraud):

1. Original manifest preserved (immutable)
2. Correction appended as new entry referencing original
3. Correction signed by corrector with reason
4. Both original and correction visible in audit trail
5. Downstream systems receive correction notification

## Revocation Handling

1. Kill switch is unconditional — creator says stop, everything stops
2. Revocation takes effect immediately (no grace period)
3. All downstream uses flagged
4. Active export packages invalidated
5. Revenue accrued before revocation is still paid to creator
6. Artist pays $0 for revocation. Ever.

## Split Override Policy

1. No split override without RSP_001 authority
2. Override must be documented with: reason, scope, date, affected parties
3. Override must not alter the public 75/25 law
4. Override applies only to internal governance (e.g., founding member 85/15)
5. Override is never disclosed in public materials

## Transition Trigger

Governance transitions from Founder to Community Council at the FIRST of:

- **50 active creators** on the platform
- **$100K gross marketplace value** (cumulative)
- **First enterprise anchor partnership** signed

At transition:
1. RSP_001 convenes election for 5-seat Community Council
2. Council includes: 2 creators, 1 legal advisor, 1 technical advisor, 1 community representative
3. RSP_001 retains veto on safety/consent issues only
4. Council governs all other policy, disputes, and revenue rules
5. Transition documented and published
