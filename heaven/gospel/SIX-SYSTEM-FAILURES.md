# THE 6 SYSTEM FAILURES NOIZY IS FIXING

> Not bad companies vs good companies.
> Unfinished systems vs artist-complete systems.

## 1. Identity Without Ownership
Voices exist without sovereignty. A voice can be cloned from seconds of audio. Identity can be copied faster than it can be protected.

**NOIZY answer:** Human Voice Sovereignty. Voice Estate. Your voice belongs to you the way your heartbeat belongs to you.

## 2. Generation Without Consent
Output happens before permission. Platforms generate first, fix later. Consent is a checkbox, not architecture.

**NOIZY answer:** Consent-as-Code. If it is not consented, it does not generate. No token, no synthesis. Structural, not optional.

## 3. Scale Without Attribution
Millions of outputs, unclear origin. The artist's work disappears into a model. No lineage. No proof. No name attached.

**NOIZY answer:** NOIZY PROOF. Every output traceable. Provenance by default, not optional watermark. SHA-256 + C2PA manifest binding.

## 4. Revenue Without Fairness
Platforms scale massively. Artists get paid on limited usage models — if they get paid at all. Value extraction without proportional return.

**NOIZY answer:** 75/25 perpetual. Real-time routing. No silent deductions. Every use of your voice pays you. Forever.

## 5. Culture Without Preservation
AI flattens culture. Accent bias. Regional identity erased. Stories, nuance, tradition compressed into "neutral" output. The human signal becomes generic.

**NOIZY answer:** Multilingual character system. Cultural specificity preserved with consent. Emotional mapping per language. The Wisdom Project captures elder genius before libraries burn.

## 6. Legacy Without Continuity
No inheritance. No lineage. When the artist dies, the voice dies. No mechanism for family to benefit. No structure for craft to continue.

**NOIZY answer:** Voice Estate is inheritable. INHERIT decision state. Beneficiary chain. Griot Fund. Your echo never stops. Your family never starves. Your art never dies.
