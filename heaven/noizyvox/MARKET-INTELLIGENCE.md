## Pillar 1: Governance & Rights

### What Works
- **Splice**: Strict rights clearance. Certified license for every download. Clear terms.
- **Soundful**: Only licensed data. "Fairly Trained" certified. Artist collabs with signed agreements.
- **Mubert**: 2.5M licensed samples. Now moving to blockchain provenance (Polkadot).

### What Fails
- **Boomy**: Platform retains copyright by default. Free users can't monetize. Fake streaming scandal ($10M fraud). CEO implicated as co-conspirator.
- **Suno/Udio**: Trained on copyrighted recordings. 70+ lawsuits pending. "Almost unimaginable scale" infringement.

### NOIZYVOX Advantage
- Consent-as-Code: structural, not policy
- 11 Never Clauses: hardcoded protections
- Provenance Ledger: every asset traceable
- Community Council: creators govern, not executives
- Kill switch: unconditional revocation

## Pillar 3: Creator Economics

### What Works
- **Splice**: $25M+ paid to creators by 2020. Karra: "life-changing quarterly checks."
- **Soundful**: Collabs feature pays artists rev share on every use of their sonic DNA
- **Mubert**: Pivoting to per-use micro-royalties via blockchain

### What Fails
- **Boomy**: 80/20 split sounds good but streaming pays fractions of cents. Months-long payment delays. Accounts frozen.

### NOIZYVOX Advantage
- 75/25 Plowman Standard (constitutional, not negotiable)
- 85/15 for founding members (internal governance only)
- Real-time routing via Stripe
- Voice Estate: inheritable, earning body of work
- Multiple income streams: models, packs, presets, services
