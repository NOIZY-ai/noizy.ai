## THE ROLE OF VOICE ACTORS IN THE WISDOM PROJECT

This is not about replacing human voices with AI. **This is the opposite.**

Your role is to be the human anchor that makes the AI's work meaningful.

Here is what we are building:

### 1. The Living Archive Sessions

Each wisdom subject — Doctor Benoit and those who follow — will participate in filmed, recorded capture sessions. These are intimate, unscripted conversations designed to draw out their deepest knowledge, their most profound insights, their unanswered questions.

Voice actors are needed to be the **interlocutors** — the skilled conversational partners who know how to listen, how to draw out, how to respond in ways that open doors rather than close them. This is performance. This is craft. But it is also human connection at its most essential.

### 2. The Voice Preservation Layer

Using tools like Expressive Neural Voice Cloning and MegaTTS 3, we will preserve not just the words of our subjects but the full expressive quality of their voice — the pitch, the rhythm, the emotional weight, the pauses that mean everything.

Voice actors will work alongside our audio engineers to ensure this capture is done with the same care a great sound designer brings to a film score. You understand voice in ways that engineers do not. We need both.

### 3. The Narrative Translation Layer

Once captured, the wisdom of each subject needs to be translated into forms that future audiences can access — documentaries, audio books, interactive AI conversations, educational modules, medical training programs.

Voice actors will narrate, interpret, and breathe life into this material. The goal is not cold data delivery. The goal is that a twenty-five year old medical student in 2075 listens to Doctor Benoit explain how he knew something was wrong before the instruments confirmed it — and **feels the weight of that knowledge**.

That is a performance challenge. That is your world.

### 4. The AI Voice Training Partner

Here is where it gets extraordinary.

The AI systems we are using — including Dia 1.6B for realistic dialogue generation, IndexTTS 2 for expressive speech synthesis, and Chatterbox for zero-shot expressive TTS — are trained on human voice data.

The quality of what an AI voice system can do is **directly determined by the quality of the human voice performances it learns from**.

Voice actors who contribute to the Wisdom Project will be training the AI systems that preserve human genius for future generations. Your voice, your expressiveness, your emotional range becomes part of the training data that teaches these systems what authentic human communication sounds and feels like.

This is not a small thing. **This is potentially the most significant contribution a voice actor can make to the future of human knowledge.**

## THE TECHNOLOGY YOU WILL BE WORKING WITH

These are real, operational tools available right now:

### For Capture
- **SpeechBrain 1.0** — PyTorch-based, 200+ models, real-time transcription and voice analysis
- **Video Speaker Diarization** — Separates and identifies every voice in a room with precision
- **M³AV** — Gold standard multimodal academic lecture capture framework

### For Voice Preservation
- **Kokoro TTS** — One of the highest-rated voice synthesis systems (3,210+ likes on Hugging Face)
- **Qwen3-TTS** — Custom speech generation from voice descriptions or samples, multilingual, expressive
- **Voice Clone** — Industry-standard voice cloning from minimal samples (2,595+ likes)
- **Expressive Neural Voice Cloning** — Full emotional range preservation
- **MegaTTS 3** — Advanced voice cloning with micro-inflection capture

### For Dialogue and Narration
- **Dia 1.6B** — Generates realistic dialogue from scripts with multiple speakers
- **VibeVoice** — Multi-speaker AI podcast generation from text scripts
- **IndexTTS 2** — Expressive speech synthesis
- **Chatterbox** — Zero-shot expressive TTS
