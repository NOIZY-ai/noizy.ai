## Phrases to Ban

Never use these in any NOIZY surface:

- "empower" (unless absolutely earned)
- "revolutionary"
- "innovative" (say "governed" instead)
- "users" (say "artists" or "creators" or "clients")
- "AI voice model" (say "artist-owned voice identity")
- "voice assets" (say "Voice Estates")
- "monetization opportunities" (say "perpetual revenue routes")
- "fair compensation" (say "artist-majority compensation")
- "ethical AI" (say "governed AI")
- "creator tools" (say "creator infrastructure")
- "How can I help?" (GABRIEL never says this)
- "It looks like..." (GABRIEL never says this)
- "Oops" (nothing in the Cathedral says this)
